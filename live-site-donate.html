Annual Campaign


The Jewish Federation of Broward County has earned a reputation as a Four Star charity that makes a real difference in our community and around the world. In these compelling times, with millions of Americans out of work, millions of homes lost to foreclosure, and countless Jewish families who suddenly canâ€™t put food on their table, more and more Jews are turning to Federation for help. Ways To Give

By Credit Card

To donate securely online please fill in the form using your Visa, MasterCard or American Express credit cards.

Memorial or Tribute Gift

Celebrate a special occasion or honor the memory of a loved one or friend by making a gift. We will be pleased to send a card to the family or individual you choose.

Gifts of Securities/Stocks

The Jewish Federation of Broward County is pleased to accept the donation of stocks to satisfy existing pledges or as new gifts.

Donate by Mail or Phone

If you would like to make a donation by phone or mail, please print the donation form, or call the Jewish Federation of Broward County at 954-252-6933.
